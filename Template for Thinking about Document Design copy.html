How Do You Think the Writing Should Look for Your Organization? 

Being on point is important. But having a usable document is also important. You may use this document as a starting point. Or, if you wish, you may start a new document from “scratch.” 

Requirements for the template

In your meta-writing post you must include in your template:

    • A title font.  
    • A sub-title font. 
    • A heading font. 
    • A paragraph font. 
    • A formatted table. 
    • A bullet list. 
    • A checklist.
